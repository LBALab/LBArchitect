Life Script: HIT_OBJ (command)

HIT_OBJ gives specified amount of damage to specified Actor.

Syntax:
  • HIT_OBJ actor(0 to 255 integer) damage(0 to 255 integer)
Parameters:
  • actor is the index of the target Actor (the Actor must exist in the Scene)
  • damage is the number of life points to take from the target Actor

HIT_OBJ does not causes the current Actor to perform any animations or play any sounds, but the target Actor will play its 'on getting hit' animation and make appropriate sound. The actual damage will be dealt only when it is higher or equal to the target Actor's armour (but the animation will be played regardless). If the amount of damage is higher or equal to the current Actor's amount of life points, the actor will die.
