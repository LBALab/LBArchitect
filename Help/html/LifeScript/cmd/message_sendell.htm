Life Script: MESSAGE_SENDELL (command)

MESSAGE_SENDELL shows the Sendell with Twinsen and Zoe screen.

Syntax:
  • MESSAGE_SENDELL

MESSAGE_SENDELL displays the text with mapped index of 6 (counting from 0) from the current text bank, and reads the text with associated voice.
