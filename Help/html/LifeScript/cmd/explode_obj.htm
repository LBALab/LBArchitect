Life Script: EXPLODE_OBJ (command)

EXPLODE_OBJ plays explosion animation over the specified Actor.

Syntax:
  • EXPLODE_OBJ actor(0 to 255 integer)
Parameters:
  • actor is the index of the target Actor (the Actor must exist in the Scene).

EXPLODE_OBJ displays one (random) of the explosion sprite animations over the Actor. The animation will be psitioned in the bottom part of the target Actor. No sound is played during the animation.
