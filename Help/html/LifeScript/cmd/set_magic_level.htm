Life Script: SET_MAGIC_LEVEL (command)

SET_MAGIC_LEVEL sets the Hero's Magic Level.

Syntax:
  • SET_MAGIC_LEVEL level(0 to 4 integer)
Parameters:
  • level is the new Magic Level to be set

There are five Magic Levels:
Level Magicball colour Magicball damage Melee damage Comments
0 Yellow 4 2 Hero cannot store magic, the Magicball does not bounce
1 Yellow 4 3  
2 Green 6 4  
3 Red 8 6  
4 Fire 10 8  

The higher level the more magic Hero can store, and the more damage his Magicball deals (also his melee damage in aggressive mode increases).

The Hero will be able to store Magic points only if he has the Tunic (item index: 4) in the inventory.
