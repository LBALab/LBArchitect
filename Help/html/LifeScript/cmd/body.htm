Life Script: BODY (command)
Life Script: BODY_OBJ (command)

BODY selects new 3D body for the current actor, BODY_OBJ selects new body for the Actor specified in the first parameter.

Syntax:
  • BODY index(-1 to 254 integer)
  • BODY_OBJ actor(0 to 255 integer) index(-1 to 254 integer)
Parameters:
  • index is a virtual index of the new body for the Actor. Actor must be a 3D Actor (unset Is Sprite static flag). Body with specified virtual index must exist in the Actor's File 3D Entity. index = -1 Selects no body for the Actor.
  • actor is the index of the target Actor (the Actor must exist in the Scene).
Example:
body 1 //select body 1 for the Actor body_obj 4 1 //select body 1 for Actor 4
