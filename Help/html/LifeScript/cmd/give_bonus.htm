Life Script: GIVE_BONUS (command)

GIVE_BONUS pops out the bonus configured in the Actor properties.

Syntax:
  • GIVE_BONUS ddis(0 or 1)
Parameters:
  • if ddis is set, the bonus will not be popped out again when the Actor dies (but still can be popped out again with another GIVE_BONUS command).

GIVE_BONUS makes the Actor pop out the bonus item (life, magic, money, little key or clover leaf) that has been set in its properties (see Actor properties: bonus for details). Both bonus type and quantity are defined in the Actor's properties. Nothing seems to depend on the command parameter, thus meaning of the parameter is unknown.

Magic points can pop out only if the Hero has at least first Magic Level (can store magic). Otherwise life points will pop out instead.
