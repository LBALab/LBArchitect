Life Script: ADD_FUEL (command)

ADD_FUEL increases amount of fuel units for the hero character (Actor 0) by specified value.

Syntax:
  • ADD_FUEL amount(0 to 255 integer)
Parameters:
  • amount is number of fuel units that will be added to the hero's inventory.

ADD_FUEL command gives the hero specified amount of fuel. The current amount of fuel can be tested with FUEL variable. The fuel is used to let player use vehicles only if he has got at least specified amount of fuel.

Example:
add_fuel 10 //give 10 units of fuel to the hero
