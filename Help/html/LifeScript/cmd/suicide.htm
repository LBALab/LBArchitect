Life Script: SUICIDE (command)

SUICIDE deletes the current Actor.

Syntax:
  • SUICIDE

SUICIDE deletes the Actor it is called for from the Scene without any effects, animations or bonuses (unlike killing the Actor in regular way by taking its life to zero). The Scripts for the Actor are stopped and cannot be run again. Effect of the command cannot be undone.
