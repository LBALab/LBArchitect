Life Script: GAME_OVER (command)

GAME_OVER immediately ends the game and displays the "GAME OVER" animation.

Syntax:
  • GAME_OVER

Use GAME_OVER to indicate that the Hero have lost. It will immediately break the game and show rotating "GAME OVER" model growing in the center of the screen. This also happens automatically if the Hero loses all his clover leafs and die.
