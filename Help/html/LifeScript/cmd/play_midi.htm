Life Script: PLAY_MIDI (command)

PLAY_MIDI plays specified MIDI music track.

Syntax:
  • PLAY_MIDI track_id(0 to 255 integer)
Parameters:
  • track_id is the index of a midi_xx.hqr file entry to be played

The MIDI music can be stopped with MIDI_OFF command.
