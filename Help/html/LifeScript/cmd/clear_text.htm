Life Script: CLEAR_TEXT (command)

CLEAR_TEXT paints upper half of the screen black.

Syntax:
  • CLEAR_TEXT

CLEAR_TEXT is used to clear the messages during ending credits sequence. During the sequence the game characters walk through the lower half of the screen while credit titles appear in the upper half (using TEXT command). This command is used to clear these titles.
