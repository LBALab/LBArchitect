Life Script: CAM_FOLLOW (command)

CAM_FOLLOW selects which Actor will be followed by the camera.

Syntax:
  • CAM_FOLLOW actor(0 to 255 integer)
Parameters:
  • actor is the index of the target Actor (the Actor must exist in the Scene).

The hero's Actor is always 0, so to restore default behaviour just execute CAM_FOLLOW 0.

Example:
cam_follow 3 //make the camera follow Actor 3
