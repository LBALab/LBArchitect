Life Script: GIVE_GOLD_PIECES (command)

GIVE_GOLD_PIECES causes the Hero to give out specified amount of gold pieces (pay for something).

Syntax:
  • GIVE_GOLD_PIECES amount(-32768 to 32767 integer)
Parameters:
  • amount tells how many gold pieces should be given out (usually cost of the bought item). Negative values make the command work in reverse - the Hero will receive the specified amount of money.

GIVE_GOLD_PIECES also displays an animation in the upper left screen corner which shows current Hero gold amount decreasing (or increasing) to the final value.
