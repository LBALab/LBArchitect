Life Script: BALLOON_ON (command)
Life Script: BALLOON_OFF (command)

BALLOON_ON enables displaying balloons when Actors say dialogues, BALLOON_OFF disables these balloons.

Syntax:
  • BALLOON_ON
  • BALLOON_OFF

BALLOON_ON/OFF shows/hides so-called "ballons" - small ellipses pointing to the head of the Actor that is saying the message. These balloons are often used in comic books to indicate whi is saying what. In LBA 1 balloons are shown when using MESSAGE_OBJ, SAY_MESSAGE(_OBJ), BIG_MESSAGE and ASK_CHOICE commands. BALLOON_ON/OFF commands disable/enable ballons for all Actors regardless of which Actor script they are used in.
