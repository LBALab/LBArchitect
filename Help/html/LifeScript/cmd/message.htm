Life Script: MESSAGE (command)
Life Script: MESSAGE_OBJ (command)

MESSAGE makes the Actor say the specified message and shows the text of it, MESSAGE_OBJ does the same, but for another Actor.

Syntax:
  • MESSAGE text_id(0 to 32767 integer)
  • MESSAGE_OBJ actor(0 to 255 integer) text_id(0 to 32767 integer)
Parameters:
  • text_id is the mapped index of text line to be displayed. The line will also be read by the associated voice.
  • actor is the index of the target Actor (the Actor must exist in the Scene).

The message text will be displayed in the regular 3-line box at the bottom of the screen. SAY_MESSAGE and BIG_MESSAGE provide other ways of displying the text.

When the Actor is saying his line, balloon will be shown over its head. This can be changed with the BALLOON_ON/OFF commands.

The text indexes used for this command are not the text positions in an lbt file (even subfile of text.hqr). Correct indexes are mapped in the corresponding odd subfile of the text.hqr. LBArchitect takes this into consideration when displaying list of possible indexes for this command.
