Life Script: SUB_FUEL (command)

SUB_FUEL decreases amount of fuel units for the hero character (Actor 0) by specified value.

Syntax:
  • SUB_FUEL amount(0 to 255 integer)
Parameters:
  • amount is number of fuel units that will be removed from the hero's inventory.

SUB_FUEL command takes specified amount of fuel from the Hero. The current amount of fuel can be tested with FUEL variable. The fuel is used to let player use vehicles only if he has got at least specified amount of fuel.

Example:
sub_fuel 10 //take 10 units of fuel from the hero
