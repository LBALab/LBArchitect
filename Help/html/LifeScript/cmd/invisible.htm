Life Script: INVISIBLE (command)

INVISIBLE hides or shows the Actor.

Syntax:
  • INVISIBLE switch(0 or 1)
Parameters:
  • if switch = 1 the Actor will be invisible, otherwise it will be visible.

INVISIBLE does not delete the Actor's body (unlike NO_BODY Track Script command), but just makes it invisible, like the Hidden Static Flag. The Actor will still be an obstacle, and play animations and sounds (though you will not see the animations :)).
