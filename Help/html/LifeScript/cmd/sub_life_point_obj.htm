Life Script: SUB_LIFE_POINT_OBJ (command)

SUB_LIFE_POINT_OBJ subtracts specified number of life points from specified Actor.

Syntax:
  • SUB_LIFE_POINT_OBJ actor(0 to 255 integer) points(0 to 255 integer)
Parameters:
  • actor is the index of the target Actor (the Actor must exist in the Scene)
  • points is the number of life points to be taken from the Actor

Unlike HIT_OBJ, SUB_LIFE_POINT_OBJ does not show any animations, it does its job silently. Only if the resulting number of life points for the Actor is zero or below, the Actor will die in its regular way (possibly dropping a bonus). There is no version of this command working on the current Actor (without '_OBJ' suffix), but the index of current Actor may be specified manually, also in LBArchitect self keyword can be used to reference the current Actor.
