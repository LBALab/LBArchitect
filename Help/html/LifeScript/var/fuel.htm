Life Script: FUEL (variable)

FUEL tells how many fuel units the Hero has.

Syntax:
  • if FUEL == value(0 to 255 integer)

(if may be also: or_if, swif, oneif; == may be also: !=, >, <, >=, <=)

Return value:
  • FUEL returns number of fuel units the Hero has.

FUEL can be used to test amount of fuel carried by the Hero. The amount of fuel may be increased with ADD_FUEL command, and decreased with SUB_FUEL command. The fuel is used to let player use vehicles only if he has got at least specified amount of fuel.

Example:
if fuel >= 5 //test if the Hero has at least 5 units of fuel
//perform operations
endif
