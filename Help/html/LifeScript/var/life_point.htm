Life Script: LIFE_POINT (variable)
Life Script: LIFE_POINT_OBJ (variable)

LIFE_POINT returns number of Life Points for the current Actor, LIFE_POINT_OBJ returns number of Life Points for another Actor.

Syntax:
  • if LIFE_POINT == value(0 to 255 integer)
  • if LIFE_POINT_OBJ actor(0 to 255 integer) == value(0 to 255 integer)

(if may be also: or_if, swif, oneif; == may be also: !=, >, <, >=, <=)

Parameters:
  • actor is the index of the target Actor (the Actor must exist in the Scene).
Return value:
  • LIFE_POINT returns number of Life Points of the current Actor,
  • LIFE_POINT_OBJ returns number of Life Points of the specified Actor.

Example usage: to make the enemy flee when low on life.

Examples:
if life_point > 10 //if the Actor has more than 10 Life Points
//perform operations
endif

if life_point_obj 5 <= 15 //if Actor 5 has 15 Life Points or less
//perform operations
endif
