Track Script: SAMPLE_RND (command)

SAMPLE_RND plays specified sound sample with randomly lowered pitch.

Syntax:
  • SAMPLE_RND id(0 to 32767 integer)
Parameters:
  • id is index (in the samples.hqr file) of the sound sample to play.

This command plays the specified sound sample once but with the pitch lowered down by a random value. It is random, so it may happen that the sample will be played with the original pitch.
