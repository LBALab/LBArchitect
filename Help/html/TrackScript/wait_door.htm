Track Script: WAIT_DOOR (command)

WAIT_DOOR pauses execution of the Track Script until the door is opened or closed completely.

Syntax:
  • WAIT_DOOR

This command may be used in connection with OPEN_XXX and CLOSE commands to ensure the door Actor is fully closed or opened before advancing to the further commands.

Example:
LABEL 1
SAMPLE 35 //door moving sound
CLOSE
WAIT_DOOR //wait for the door to close before executing next commands
STOP
END
