Track Script: ANGLE_RND (command)

ANGLE_RND changes angle of facing for the actor turning it in a random direction by about 90°.

Syntax:
  • ANGLE_RND max_rand(1 to 32767 integer)
Parameters
  • max_rand defines the randomness range around 90°. max_rand = 0 will crash the game, so it has been disabled in LBArchitect. The parameter is in Scene units, not degrees - see ANGLE for more information.

The parameter defines randomness range of the resulting angle around 90°. The resulting angle is calculated from equation: Rnd(±) 256 + max_rand/2 - Rnd(max_rand). Thus the center of random range is at 90°, and the value may differ from 90°-(max_rand/2) to 90°+(max_rand/2). For example max_rand = 6 will turn the Actor left or right by ± 90 ± 1 degrees, while max_rand = 256 will turn the Actor by ± 90 ± 45 degrees.

ANGLE_RND will make the actor "visually" turn around from the old angle to the new one.

Rotation speed is determined by the Rotation delay Actor property.

Example:
angle_rnd 256
