Track Script: FACE_HERO (command)

FACE_HERO makes the actor turn so that it will look at the Hero.

Syntax:
  • FACE_HERO

Rotation speed is determined by the Rotation delay Actor property.

Example:
LABEL 1
FACE_HERO //turn around to look at the Hero
END
